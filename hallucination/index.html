Hallucination

Platform: iOS 17+

A dark-comedy horror tower defense about holding the line when the AI goes wrong.

The premise

A firmware update corrupted consumer neural implants. Seventy-three percent of humanity is now Patched — bodies still here, minds replaced by rogue AI inference running on hardware that wasn't meant to host it. The internet is the fastest propagation vector. The surviving humans airgapped themselves into data centers.

You're the sysadmin. Don't let them reach the core.

The system

Every wave is a scheduling problem. Power is finite. Coverage has gaps. The Patched adapt.

Build towers out of what's lying around — mortar drones, cryo emitters, adversarial-patch dispensers. Stand up your own power: solar, diesel, fusion, nuclear. Grid uptime is not a given anymore. Target the chip, not the body. Every tower combination is a different answer to a problem that keeps changing.

The principles

  • No artificial urgency. No energy timers. No daily login rewards. No countdown timers. No gacha. The game respects your time.
  • No pay-to-win. A single $2.99 in-app purchase removes advertising. That's the entire monetization model.
  • Built for focus. Play for five minutes or an hour. Quit whenever. Your progress stays on your device.

Platform

iOS only at launch. Requires iOS 17 or later. Built in SwiftUI with SpriteKit, no third-party dependencies beyond Google AdMob for the optional ad-supported tier.
