This Privacy Policy describes how Eighth Ventures LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of OurBar (the "App"). By using OurBar, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information generated through your use of the App, and limited technical information from your device.

Information You Provide

Data Type Examples Purpose
Account information Name, email address, password Create and manage your account
Profile information Display name, avatar Personalize your experience
Bar inventory Ingredients you add to your bar Power cocktail matching and sharing
Cocktail activity Ratings, notes, favorites Track your preferences and history
Social activity Groups joined, events created, Flights participated in Enable group and social features
Spirit discoveries Spirits logged, tasting notes, scores Power the Discoveries feature

Information Generated Automatically

  • Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • App version and crash reports
  • General usage patterns (screens visited, features used)
  • IP address and approximate location (country/region level only)
Important
We do not collect precise GPS location, payment information, contacts, health data, or any information from your device outside the App.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide and improve the App — match cocktails to your inventory, power group features, and improve recommendations
  • Personalize your experience — remember your preferences, ratings, and bar inventory across sessions
  • Enable social features — share inventory with groups, participate in Flights, send Discoveries to friends
  • Communicate with you — send notifications about group activity, Flight updates, and Discovery reveals
  • Ensure safety and security — detect fraud, investigate abuse, and enforce our Terms of Use
  • Analyze and improve performance — understand how the App is used and fix bugs
  • Comply with legal obligations — respond to lawful requests and applicable law

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

How We Share Your Information

With Other Users

OurBar is a social app. Certain information is visible to other users by design:

  • Your display name and avatar are visible to users in your groups and friends list
  • Your bar inventory is visible to group members when you share it for a session
  • Your Discoveries tasting notes and scores are revealed to specific recipients you choose, only after they log their own score for the same spirit
  • Your Flight scores and rankings are visible to group members after a bracket round concludes

You control what you share. Private inventory items and personal notes are never shared without your explicit action.

With Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the App:

Provider Purpose Data Shared
Supabase Database, authentication, and file storage All user data stored in the App
Expo / EAS App distribution and over-the-air updates Device identifiers, app version
Apple App distribution via the App Store As required by Apple's platform policies

All service providers are contractually obligated to use your information only to provide services to us and in accordance with this policy.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers operated by Supabase, Inc. with data centers in the United States. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Encrypted data at rest
  • Row-level security policies limiting data access
  • Authentication via Supabase Auth with secure session management

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specifically:

  • Account data — retained until you delete your account
  • Bar inventory and ratings — retained until you delete your account or remove individual items
  • Group and social data — retained until you leave a group or delete your account
  • Usage logs — retained for up to 90 days for debugging and security purposes

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal information and account
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out of notifications — disable push notifications at any time through your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@eighthventures.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You may delete your account at any time from within the App under Settings → Account → Delete Account.

Children's Privacy

OurBar is intended for users of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction. The App is not directed to children under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a minor without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately at privacy@eighthventures.com.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell
  • The right to delete personal information we have collected from you
  • The right to opt-out of the sale of personal information — we do not sell personal information
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To submit a California privacy rights request, contact us at privacy@eighthventures.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy and, where appropriate, sending a notification through the App.

Your continued use of OurBar after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
